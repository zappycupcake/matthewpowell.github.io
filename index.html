Dynamic A/B Testing of New Naked Wines Branding

Designer & Developer

This year, Naked Wines began the process of rebranding. With the need to understand it's impact, we rebranded an exisiting customer journey to A/B test.

With pressure to test quickly, the marketing team intially wanted to to build one page with one product. However, I saw the opportunity to build a dynamic page; across multiple products which would help us A/B test further in the future. It also gave the digital team autonomy; allowing them to manage the pages themselves via our CMS and freeing up our design and tech teams. Win-win!

Advent Calendar

Designer & Developer

Naked Wines first ever Christmas Calendar! Our competitors, Virgin Wines and Laithwaites had their own similar campaigns, but we wanted to produce something that stood out and offered more.

24 independent winemakers offering 24 incredible wines to be opened over December; unwrap, pour and then scan then the back of the wine carton of more information about the winemaker and food pairings.

I designed and built the landing page, using existing components from our design library.

The campaign was a success! We sold out within the first 24 hours of the pre-orders going on sale.

Naked Wines Email Design System

Designer & Developer

When I joined Naked Wines, there was a lack of parity and visual consistency across all of our global email communication.

There was a clear business need to improve the design, build quality and accessiablity of our emails. Implementing a email design system consistent with our brand would save time and increase our email marketing output.

By creating an extensive design system and defining templates, image and copy size, I created and built a suite of flexible components that enabled us to test across multiple email clients quickly and efficiently.

Save for Christmas

Designer & Developer

Naked Wines wanted to lock customers' Christmas orders in, before the wine tax duty increase. I designed, managed and built the campaign. The challenge here was to clearly and concisely visually explain the payment plans and, depending on the customer segment, dynamically display the five different case products on offer.

A huge commercial success for Naked Wines beating the forecast with 10,000+ pre-orders within the first week.
